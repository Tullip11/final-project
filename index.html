Wichita Earth Crew is a collective of individuals working with each other, organizations, businesses, associations, & groups to create connected spaces that allow us to have a positive impact on our community. We strive to do our work through an intersectional environmentalist lens, centering historically excluded voices (BIPOC, disabled, LGBTQ+, etc.) understanding how identity impacts experiences & access to opportunity and working towards equity for all.
