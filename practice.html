Wichita Earth Crew is a collective of individuals working with each other, organizations, businesses, associations, & groups to create connected spaces that allow us to have a positive impact on our community.

We strive to do our work through an intersectional environmentalist lens, ensuring visibility and centering historically excluded voices by understanding how identity impacts experience & access to opportunity and working towards equity for all.

Unfamiliar with the terms intersectionality or intersectional environmentalism? Check out the works of Kimberlé Crenshaw, who coined the term 'intersectionality' in 1989, + Leah Thomas who coined the term ‘intersectional environmentalism' in 2020.
